## What The Hero Needs To Communicate

SSAI is not a dashboard, chatbot, or alerting layer. It is a cognitive DevOps engineer that joins a small engineering team, learns the stack, does operational work, and leaves evidence. The visitor should understand that the value is judgment plus inspectability: SSAI acts, but the team can audit the reasoning.

The hero should speak to technical founders and engineering leaders who are already carrying DevOps responsibility. The copy should feel direct, competent, and adult. No hype, no promises that sound magical, no vague AI language.

## Copy Principles

- Lead with the missing role, not the technology.
- Make autonomy credible by pairing it with evidence.
- Avoid dashboard, monitoring, or chatbot framing.
- Keep verbs operational: self-onboards, investigates, rolls back, renews, traces, suppresses, documents.
- Keep the emotional pitch quiet: fewer pages, fewer interruptions, more handled work.
- Use short declarative sentences. No exclamation marks.
- Avoid em dashes.
